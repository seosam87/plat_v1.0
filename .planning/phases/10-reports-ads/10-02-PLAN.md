       - Use `weasyprint.HTML(string=rendered_html, base_url="app/static/").write_pdf()`.
       - Wrap the synchronous WeasyPrint call in `asyncio.get_event_loop().run_in_executor(None, ...)`.
       - Return PDF bytes.
