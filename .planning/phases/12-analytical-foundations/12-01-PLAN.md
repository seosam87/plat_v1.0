<objective>
Create the two infrastructure foundations that all Phase 12 analytical queries depend on: (1) a `normalize_url()` utility that unifies URLs for correct JOINs between pages, metrika_traffic_pages, and keyword_positions; (2) a `keyword_latest_positions` flat table that replaces expensive DISTINCT ON partition scans with a simple indexed lookup.

Purpose: Without normalize_url(), JOINs between pages (which store URLs like `https://example.com/page/`) and metrika_traffic_pages (which may store `http://example.com/page?utm_source=yandex`) silently return zero matches. Without keyword_latest_positions, every Quick Wins or Dead Content query requires scanning all monthly partitions of keyword_positions with DISTINCT ON, taking 8-15s at 100K keywords.

Output: normalize_url utility, KeywordLatestPosition model, Alembic migration, position_service integration, tests
</objective>
